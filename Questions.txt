1. In 1-2 sentences, define end to end testing.

Once the developers are confident that the app is a cohesive whole, end to end testing can begin. The process involves a battery of tests that attempt to duplicate almost all possible user interactions with the app to identify bugs and help delivery a smoother experience to the end point user.
